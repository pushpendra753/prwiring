FAQ

Do you provide free site visits and quotations? +
Yes! We provide free site visits for projects in Raipur and Bhilai. After visiting, we give you a detailed, written quotation at no charge and with no obligation.
How quickly can you start work after booking? +
For urgent repairs, we can often come the same day or next day. For larger projects, we typically schedule within 2–5 days depending on availability. Call us to confirm.
What areas do you serve? +
We primarily serve Raipur and Bhilai and all surrounding areas including Durg, Rajnandgaon, and nearby localities. Call us to confirm if we cover your specific area.
Do you provide a warranty on your work? +
Yes. All workmanship is guaranteed. If any issue arises due to our work, we will fix it at no additional cost. We use quality ISI-marked materials on every job.

Absolutely. We handle everything from a single switch replacement to complete factory electrical installations. Every job gets the same professional attention.
