FAQ

Common Questions

Do you provide free site inspection?+
Yes — free site visit for estimation within Raipur & Bhilai. For other areas, a nominal visit charge applies, adjusted in the final bill.
How long does house wiring take?+
Standard 2–3 BHK wiring takes 7–12 working days depending on size & scope. Clear timeline given before work starts.
Do you use ISI certified materials?+
Absolutely — ISI marked wires, MCBs, switches. Brands: Havells, Polycab, Legrand, Anchor — as per your budget.
Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contract?+
Yes — AMC for homes, offices & industries. Includes periodic inspection, preventive maintenance & priority breakdown support. Contact us for pricing.
Do you work outside Raipur?+
Yes — Raipur, Bhilai, Durg, Rajnandgaon, Bilaspur & nearby areas. Travel allowance may apply for remote locations.
Is proper earthing really necessary?+
Yes! IS 3043 earthing is mandatory and is your primary protection against electric shock. We never skip earthing — it's non-negotiable for us.
Do you handle emergency breakdowns?+
Yes — 24/7 emergency support available. Call or WhatsApp 7354283083 for urgent electrical breakdowns, tripping, short circuits.
